Student Resources

Student Success Center

Bay Mills Community College provides a number of student services to assist students to succeed in a college environment. Programs of study at BMCC are designed to provide opportunities for students to develop skills and experience in chosen areas. Students can complete programs of study preparing them to transfer to a four-year institution or to seek immediate employment.

Programs and Courses

The Associate of Arts and the Associate of Science degree programs are BMCC's basic academic transfer programs. Courses in these programs form the basis for many career options and majors and parallel those offered in the first two years at four-year baccalaureate degree institutions.

Bachelor Programs
BMCC has three Bachelor of Science programs

Early Childhood Education

Information Technology

Business Administration

The Early Childhood Education program is designed to prepare teachers, family service, education managers, and administrators of Indian Head Start facilities nationwide, which is the primary focus of these programs.

The Business Administration program will give students a strong foundation in grants, management, marketing, and finance, with a focus on working in tribal communities. Students who are in BMCC’s associate degree in business administration major can seamlessly transition into the bachelor’s degree program as they move forward.

The New Information Technology Program is designed to build institutional capacity through increased computer science education that strengthens tribal sovereignty. The program will be offered fully online and is designed for students nationwide.
